About Us


Welcome to neel! At neel, we are passionate about delivering the finest and freshest dairy products straight to your doorstep. We take great pride in sourcing our products from local, trusted farms that share our commitment to quality, sustainability, and animal welfare.

Our Missions


Our mission is simple: to provide you with the highest quality dairy products while supporting local farmers and promoting environmentally friendly practices. We believe in the power of nature and the goodness it offers, which is why we strive to offer products that are free from artificial additives and preservatives.
